Diabetes Mellitus

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs either when the pancreas does not produce enough insulin or when the body cannot effectively use the insulin it produces.

463M People affected worldwide
1.5M Deaths annually
Check Your Risk

Cardiovascular Disease

Heart disease refers to various types of conditions that can affect heart function, including coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and heart defects.

17.9M Deaths annually
85% Due to heart attack/stroke
Check Your Risk

Chronic Kidney Disease

Chronic kidney disease means your kidneys are damaged and can't filter blood the way they should. This can lead to waste buildup in your body.

850M People affected worldwide
1.2M Deaths annually
