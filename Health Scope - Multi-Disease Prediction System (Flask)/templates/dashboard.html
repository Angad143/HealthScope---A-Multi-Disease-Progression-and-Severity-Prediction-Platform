Diabetes Mellitus

Metabolic Disorder

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs when the pancreas doesn't produce enough insulin or when the body cannot effectively use the insulin it produces. This leads to elevated blood glucose levels.

463M People affected worldwide
1.5M Deaths annually
50% Undiagnosed cases

Cardiovascular Disease

Circulatory System

Heart disease refers to various conditions affecting the heart and blood vessels, including coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and congenital heart defects.

17.9M Deaths annually
85% Due to heart attack/stroke
80% Preventable cases

Chronic Kidney Disease

Renal System

Chronic kidney disease occurs when kidneys become damaged and can't filter blood effectively, leading to waste buildup and other complications.

850M People affected worldwide
1.2M Deaths annually
90% Unaware of condition
