Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Health Scope is a multi-disease progression and severity prediction platform that uses machine learning to help users monitor their health status and predict potential risks for various diseases.

Please find below the steps for administrators to use the Health Scope system:

  1. Log into the System using your account to start generating risk assessments
  2. Select the type of disease for which you want to generate risks
  3. Input the Patient's Vitals
  4. Click on Submit
  5. Get Assessment Result
  6. Download as PDF

Our predictions are based on validated machine learning models trained on large datasets. While they provide valuable insights, they should not replace professional medical advice.
